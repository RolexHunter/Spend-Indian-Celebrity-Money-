About Us

MoneySpend is a virtual shopping simulator where you can experience what it's like to spend the fortunes of India's top cricketers, YouTubers, and business moguls.

Our goal is to blend entertainment, finance, and fantasy — allowing users to explore, imagine, and interact with celebrity-level wealth in a fun and educational way.

Built with love for cricket fans, digital creators, and number nerds — we bring you an engaging platform with 100+ products and realistic net worth simulations.
